Welcome to PickPic!

Welcome to PickPic, the app designed by Inutil Labs Inc. for your enjoyment! PickPic is a user-friendly application that allows you to access publicly shared images from Social Media and link them to your personal contacts.

Using PickPic

No 3rd Party Applications Required: PickPic operates independently from all your applications and does not require access to the apps installed on your device.

Privacy Assurance: PickPic respects your privacy. We do not gather any data from the app user nor do we share anything with any third party.

Access to Contacts: The app will, upon the user approval, access and list the phone's contacts to, for the user selected contacts, find pictures that the user can (or not) add to the contact.
